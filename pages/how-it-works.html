SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

From raw signals to stable frames

PulseBoost operates as a closed-loop optimization system. It observes, predicts, acts — and stays reversible. Below is the pipeline, plus diagnostics and explainability outputs you can copy and share.

Optimization pipeline

STEP 01Telemetry ingestion
Low-overhead signals via native Windows APIs: scheduling, GPU queues, memory pressure, pacing.
STEP 02Baseline modeling
Learns your normal band. Deviations matter more than raw usage numbers.
STEP 03Bottleneck inference
Predicts the likely cause: CPU contention, queue starvation, background interference.
STEP 04Reversible action
Applies the smallest safe change, constrained by guardrails and rollback.

Safety & control model

Rollback always
Every action is tracked and can be reverted instantly.
No driver hacks
No unsigned drivers. No kernel hooks. No injection.
Bounded envelopes
Models operate inside safe action ranges. Out-of-policy actions are rejected.
